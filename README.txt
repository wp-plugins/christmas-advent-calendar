== Description ==

A simple but highly enjoyable add-on to your Wordpress website. Get a list of randomly drawn christmas advent calendars from julekalender.com. Participate in all sorts of competitions and win great prices.
Share the experience with all of your users.

The plugin currently supports english, norwegian, danish and swedish.

== Installation ==

1. Upload Christmas Advent Calendar to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or simply download it from Wordpress.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Open widgets section and drag it into your preferred widget area
1. Select number of calendars to show (calendars are randomly drawn from our calendar pool to keep your plugin-area constantly changing)
1. Participate and win prices. (you might want to bookmark the ones like enjoy the most)
1. Buy it!
